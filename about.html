About the brand

Local media with somewhere useful to go.

Delaware Beach Finds is the audience-facing discovery brand of Rent Delaware Beaches, LLC—built to turn curiosity into local exploration, trust and commerce.

Why this exists

Most travel pages either feel like generic tourism brochures or endless lists built for clicks. Delaware Beach Finds is meant to feel more like the friend who actually pays attention: what is worth the drive, what fits your mood, what to pack and where to go next.

The brand starts at the Delaware beaches, but the curiosity extends naturally through Delmarva, South Jersey, southeastern Pennsylvania and the broader Mid-Atlantic.

Brand roles

Delaware Beach Finds

Media, discovery, Instagram, local guides and audience trust.

Rent Delaware Beaches, LLC

The company and operating infrastructure behind the project.

The Blue Hen Basement

The Etsy creative-product channel for gifts, downloads and original designs.

Editorial promise

Useful first. Monetized second.

No fake firsthand claims

Guides distinguish personal perspective, research and recommendations instead of pretending every place was secretly “discovered.”

No junk-catalog energy

Products belong when they solve a real problem or express genuine local identity.

Local credit matters

Photographers, businesses and community contributors should be named and linked whenever possible.
