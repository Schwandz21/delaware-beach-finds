About

A weekly look at the Delaware coast.

Delaware Beach Finds started as @delawarebeachfinds on Instagram — sunrise photos, the occasional beach dog, a lot of "where is this" comments. This is the version of it you can sit with a little longer.

Every week gets a feature story, a hidden gem, and whatever the weekend calendar happens to be. None of it is written to sell you something first — the towns, the tides and the people who show up in this feed are the actual point.

The shop exists because a few people kept asking where the prints in certain photos came from. It stays small on purpose.

Submissions run entirely through Instagram — tag a photo, get featured, no forms to fill out. If you want to reach us directly, say hello here.

Delaware Beach Finds is an independent publication. It shares an owner with Rent Delaware Beaches, a separate trip-planning resource — but this is where the coast gets covered for its own sake.
