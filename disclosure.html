Affiliate & sponsorship disclosure

How Delaware Beach Finds handles monetized recommendations.

Delaware Beach Finds is designed to earn revenue through products, advertising, sponsorships and potentially affiliate links.

Owned stores

Links to The Blue Hen Basement on Etsy and the connected Shopify store promote products associated with Rent Delaware Beaches, LLC or its brands. The business may earn revenue from purchases.

Affiliate links

Some future links may generate a commission when a reader buys something, without increasing the reader’s price. Qualifying links should be identified in the article or near the link.

Sponsored content

Paid collaborations should be labeled clearly. Payment does not guarantee a positive opinion, and audience relevance should control whether a partnership is accepted.

Editorial standard

The goal is useful first, monetized second. Readers should buy only what fits their needs and independently verify product details.
