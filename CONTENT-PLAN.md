# 90-Day SEO + Instagram Commerce Plan

## Core funnel
Instagram creates attention → SEO article resolves intent → contextual product CTA sends the visitor to Shopify or Etsy → email and Instagram bring the visitor back.

## Month 1: Delaware beach authority
Publish or deepen:
- Rehoboth vs. Dewey vs. Bethany comparisons
- Cape Henlopen guides
- Rainy-day guides
- Packing and parking problem-solving pages
- Town-specific weekend itineraries

Instagram support:
- One Reel hook per article
- One carousel summarizing the decision framework
- Stories linking to the article and asking a question

## Month 2: Commerce clusters
Build pages around:
- Delaware beach-house gifts
- Personalized town and coordinates products
- Beach-day kits
- Quick-dry and sand-control products
- Rental welcome templates and trip planners

Each product should have:
- One commercial guide
- One use-case article
- Three short-form social angles
- One Pinterest pin set

## Month 3: Regional expansion
Add intentional day-trip clusters:
- Cape May and South Jersey
- Assateague and Ocean City
- Delmarva small towns
- Brandywine Valley / southeastern Pennsylvania weekend finds

## Keyword principles
Prioritize phrases that reveal a decision or problem:
- best Delaware beach for families
- Rehoboth vs Bethany Beach
- things to do near Delaware beaches when it rains
- Cape Henlopen day trip itinerary
- Delaware beach packing list
- Delaware beach house gifts
- Cape May day trip from Delaware

Avoid publishing thin “10 best” lists unless the page includes a real framework, original media, current verification and useful internal links.
